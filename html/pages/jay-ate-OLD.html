1999 by Jay Ate

Cover art for Jay Ate's Dec 2017 EP, 1999. Lo-fi hiphop beats over obscure samples, this album is soaked in an aura of spacey nostalgia. To help illustrate this feeling, producer Josh Parrot and I agreed on a simillar approach with the art. A spacy coloage of "sampled" retro images. The end result is a longated vertical colage that extends upward from the front to the back when flipped forward. Otherwise the back image is upside-down, or is it just your perspective that's flipped?
