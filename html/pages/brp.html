Basic Research Press is the pet project of Dr. Joseph M. Brown, a retired mechanical engineering professor from Mississippi State University. Before becoming a teacher he worked for McDonnell Douglas developing aircraft, missiles, & satellites. At one point, while working for McDonnell Douglas, he was tasked with a group to work on developing a working Unified Theory of Physics. Something he continued to work on in his spare time since.
