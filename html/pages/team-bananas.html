Team Bananas Logos | Branding | Pandemic Project

With 2020 being absolutely awful & everyone stuck inside, I thought it'd be a great time to do some updated branding for my team of friends. Despite it's retro, nostalgic charm, the old logo made with MSPaint just wasn't really doing it anymore.


After updating the original 2004 logo, I started working on other variations & designs to be made into stickers to send out to the team. Somewhere in there beer was made.
